Medicaid GLP-1 Obesity Drug Coverage: State-by-State Evidence Sources

This page documents the evidence behind each state's coverage classification for GLP-1 receptor agonist drugs indicated for obesity treatment under Medicaid fee-for-service.

Drugs covered: Wegovy (semaglutide), Saxenda (liraglutide 3.0 mg), Zepbound (tirzepatide).

Last updated: March 20, 2026.
